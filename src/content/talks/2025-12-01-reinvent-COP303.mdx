Join me, **Damini Kumar**, **Ganesh Balkumar**, **Mitu Bir** and **Udaya Jaladi** for this interactive Builders' session where we'll explore how to strengthen mission-critical workloads through automated disaster recovery planning.

<TalkImage src="/talks/cop303.png" alt="COP303 - Automate disaster recovery playbooks using generative AI" title="AWS re:Invent 2025" />

In this session, I'll demonstrate practical techniques using Amazon Q Developer, AWS Resilience Hub, and AWS Systems Manager to generate and validate recovery runbooks:

- Craft effective prompts for architecture-aware recovery plans
- Generate and validate disaster recovery runbooks automatically
- Modernize your disaster recovery strategy with generative AI
- Reduce risk and improve operational resilience through automation
